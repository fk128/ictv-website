ICTV is the Imperial Union's TV station and film-making society! We are here to produce short films, documentaries, music videos, sketches and much more! We give Imperial Students the chance to present, direct, write, edit and produce all sorts of programming. Even if you've never touched a camera before, we are here to help! We have lots of high quality equipment and want it to be used on a wide variety of student projects, so if you're interested in creating content, being involved in any film project, or just interested in the technical side of broadcasting and film production, then do get in touch with us and join ICTV. At ICTV you also get the chance to meet other people that are interested in media. There will be a variety of tutorial sessions run throughout the year that will teach you everything from how to operate a camera to creating and editing good quality content.
